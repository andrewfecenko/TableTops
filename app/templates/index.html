What We Do

TableTop looks to turn office spaces into communities of likeminded individuals or small businesses. If you are looking for a place to get inspired, meet people with similar interests and get your independent work done then you’ve come to the right place. Coworking is about sharing you work environment while working independently. Take a look around and you’ll find plenty of spaces in your area that you can become a part of.

If you are a land lord and want to open up a new cowering space or are just looking to fill a few seats then TableTop will help you get there. Create a new office space, upload some pictures and we’ll expose you to hundreds of tenants looking for office space.
